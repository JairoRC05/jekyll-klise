LIGA INDIGO CAMPEONES

0/18

JULIO

The first-ever edition of the UEFA European Championship.

1964

Spain wins on home soil in 1964 in duel of political perspectives.

1984

The 1984 tournament is generally regarded as the first Women's Euro.

1988

Marco van Basten's volley seals the Oranje's first major title.

1992

The first edition in which players have their names on their shirts.

1996

Germany wins thanks to the first golden goal ever in a major tournament.

2000

The first tournament to be hosted by two countries, the Netherlands and Belgium.

2004

Dismissed as rank outsiders before the tournament, Greece sensationally wins.

2008

Klasnić scores for Croatia at the finals, 15 months after two kidney transplants.

2012

Spain becomes the only team to retain the title, after winning in 2008.

2016

The tournament expands to include 24 teams, up from the 16-team format.

2020

UEFA Euro 2020 is postponed until 2021 due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

2024

Germany hosts the 17th edition of the UEFA European Championship.

2028

UEFA Euro 2028 will take place in the UK and Republic of Ireland.
